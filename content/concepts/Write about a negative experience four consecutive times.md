# Write about a negative experience four consecutive times to decrease emotionality

Write for 15-30 minutes, no worry about grammar or anyone reading it. People find that on average, the pattern of language shifts over the sessions. This helps detach the emotion of the experience.

The prefrontal cortex is activated, making people more honest. This leads to neuroplasticity.
