# Zettelkasten is about simplicity

In note taking, folder structure is only necessary once notes start to build up, and even then, it's not for finding things. It's just to keep the workspace clean. 

By being a [[Technology is built to solve a problem, but when it isn't also built to be beautiful as well, we will occasionally suffer from it|beautiful]] system, it's less likely to cause suffering and instead support the task at hand, that is note taking.
