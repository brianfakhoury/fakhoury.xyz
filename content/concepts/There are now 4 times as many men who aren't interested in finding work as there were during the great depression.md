# There are now 4 times as many men who aren't interested in finding work as there were during the great depression

Roughly 7 million men in the US are not employed, nor are looking for employment. This number has only risen during the past century. It's not enough to look at unemployment numbers as it doesn't capture this group of men who don't want to find work.
