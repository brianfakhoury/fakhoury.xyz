# Distance yourself from feelings to avoid madness

The idea is that to fully feel all of life's feelings would be overwhelming, essentially impossible to do. The ability to, over time, look at things in the past rationally and objectively lets you avoid a sense of difficulty and urgency.

This further reflects that mental illness has the property of not being able to properly sequence thoughts, but instead everything coming with urgency and worry.
