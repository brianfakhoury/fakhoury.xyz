# A high trust society has mostly virtuous people which benefits everyone

When virtues, such as honesty, are prevalent, things like business become easier. People trust each other to show up, which in the stag hunt game, increases the average outcome. Even if some cheat, as long as there aren't too many, enough cooperation is possible to benefit everyone.
