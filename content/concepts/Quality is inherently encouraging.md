# Quality is inherently encouraging

[[Understanding quality leads to gumption]], and therefore quality is an inherent source of motivation. In contrast, [[Glorifying oneself as a means of motivation is a bad idea]]. This lying to oneself is why [[Associating with innate talent makes you weak]].

Letting go of ego and placing your sights on excellence, while significantly harder, puts you in a mental state to deal with and seek truth. This then serves as foundation to training, learning, and living.
