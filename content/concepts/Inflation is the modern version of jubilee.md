# Inflation is the modern version of jubilee

The historical [[jubilee]] would not work in today's conplex world, but debt must be washed away somehow. Inflation is the modern equivalent of this process by which the currency that debt is denominated in inflates away the price of the debt over time. This keeps things in check to some extent.
