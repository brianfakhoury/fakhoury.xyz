# Food choice based on omega-6 content

**Foods to Bias Towards:**
1. **Fats and Oils:**
   - MCT Oil
   - Beef Tallow (Grass-Fed)
   - Grass-Fed Butter
   - Palm Kernel Oil
   - Coconut Oil (Regular and Refined)
   - Ghee (Clarified Butter)
2. **Meat, Seafood, Tofu, and Eggs:**
   - Grass-Fed Ground Beef
   - Goat
   - Grass-Fed Beef Sirloin Steak
   - Leg of Lamb
   - Pastured Eggs
   - Wild Seafood (e.g., Wild Coho or Silver Salmon, Pink Salmon)
   - Low Omega-6 Tofu (Silken Tofu, Firm Tofu)
3. **Grains, Beans, and Legumes/Pulses:**
   - Green Beans, Green Peas
   - Navy Beans, Cooked
   - Whole Grains (e.g., White Rice, Buckwheat Groats)
4. **Fruits and Vegetables:**
   - Almost all fruits and vegetables (e.g., Sweet Corn, Fresh Coconut, Black Olives)
5. **Nuts and Seeds:**
   - Macadamia Nuts
   - Chestnuts
   - Chia Seeds
   - Flax Seeds
6. **Chocolate:**
   - Dark or Unsweetened Chocolate
7. **Dairy:**
   - Grass-Fed Milk
   - Greek Yogurt (Nonfat, Plain)
   - Grass-Fed Cheese (e.g., Feta, Brie, Blue Cheese)
8. **Condiments:**
   - Soy Sauce
   - Fish Sauce
   - Mustard

**Foods to Bias Away From:**

1. **Fats and Oils:**
   - Sunflower Oil
   - Soybean Oil
   - Corn Oil
   - Grapeseed Oil
   - Safflower Oil
2. **Meat, Seafood, Tofu, and Eggs:**
   - Conventional Pork Spare Ribs
   - Conventional Chicken Wings
   - Farmed Salmon
   - Conventional Eggs
3. **Grains, Beans, and Legumes/Pulses:**
   - Edamame (Soybeans)
   - High Omega-6 Flours (e.g., Almond Flour, Soy Flour)
4. **Fruits and Vegetables:**
   - High Omega-6 Vegetables (e.g., Avocados)
5. **Nuts and Seeds:**
   - Walnuts
   - Pine Nuts
   - Sunflower Seeds
6. **Dairy:**
   - Soy Milk
   - Almond Milk
   - Rice Milk
7. **Condiments:**
   - Store-Bought Ranch Dressing
   - Store-Bought Blue Cheese Dressing
   - Conventional Mayonnaise
