# Keep track of the most important questions to create a mental game tape

From Josh's MIQ process, you need to create a game tape in your mind to play back and learn from. You can do this by leaving the most important question in your mind after training. The shift in what you focus on over the course of days is the gap, and this gap analysis is an important meta-learning process.
