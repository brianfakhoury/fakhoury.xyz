# Memes trickle down into harder mediums

[[If it's important, it will end up in a book]], which we assume is a harder medium than social media. This is because there is more friction to writing and distributing a book, and for it to be popular it must be constantly spreading because it is a good book to read.

This is also why we need to [[Use digital platforms to share genuine, real-world efforts]], as real-world efforts are a better source of input material that has a higher chance of making it to harder mediums.
