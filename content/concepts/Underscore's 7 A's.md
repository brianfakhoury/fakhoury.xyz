# Underscore's 7 A's

When evaluating a startup in the early stages, you don't look at the startup as it doesn't really exist. You evaluate the founder. A strong founder has grit and endurance. These founders are able to increase the company lows regardless of market conditions. The highs are met with careful optimism, yet the lows don't stop them from continuing.
