# Localized teaching methods are not as broadly applicable as life principles

One can learn or teach anything as effectively with domain specific language as they can with life principles. For example, in chess one can learn by the book and the jargon, or they can learn by principles of how to live and fight. The latter is more likely to transfer to other areas of life.
