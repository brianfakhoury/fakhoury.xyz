# If you were not able to distract yourself right now, what might you need to think about or feel?

We engage in obsessive behavior to avoid painful or difficult emotions. Often something is waiting there to be dealt with if you just stop and force yourself to think about or feel it. When you scroll compulsively for hours on your phone, what might you be avoiding that should be dealt with?
