# Distance your wellbeing from your circumstance

[[Psychological distancing as a technique to better deal with stress]] is a well studied phenomena that improves performance under stress. Perhaps it's not the only way to do well, but it's along the same vain as [[Circumstance has nothing to do with you wellbeing]].

The ability to perform or execute something is only loosely coupled to your state of being, if it's physically or mentally possible, then the last thing you want is to get in the way of yourself.
