# The secret to goal pursuit is to have both delayed and immediate rewards

Combining these two is like "rocket fuel" for endurance. This is why intrinsic motivation is important. Setting long term goals is easy when you're an observer, but in the moment, those long term goals are not enough to drive you to push through hardships. The opposite of this would be [[Slave mentality is only doing work when whipped or when a carrot is present]].
